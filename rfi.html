Accepts multiple PDF files — existing annotations and markup notes are extracted automatically.

How to export your markup list from Bluebeam Revu:
  1. Open your marked-up PDF in Bluebeam Revu.
  2. Open the Markups List panel (View → Panels → Markups List, or press Alt+L).
  3. Click the Export button (grid/table icon) at the top of the Markups List panel.
  4. Choose Export Summary to CSV — or choose Export to XML for full coordinate data.
  5. Save the file, then drop it below.
Both CSV and XML exports are supported. Subject, Comments, Page, Color, and Type fields are mapped automatically.
